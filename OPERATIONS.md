# VeriAfrica – Operations Guide

## Founder Responsibilities (Early Stage)

### Daily Tasks (5–15 minutes)
- Review top content via the dashboard
- Remove inappropriate or inaccurate items
- Monitor trending categories

---

### Weekly Tasks
- Analyze engagement metrics
- Identify high-performing content types
- Adjust source prioritization if necessary

---

## Long-Term Focus
- Platform growth
- Strategic partnerships
- Funding and sustainability
- Product direction

The platform is designed to operate with minimal manual intervention.
