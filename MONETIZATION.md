# MONETIZATION.md
Project: VeriAfrica  
Purpose: Define how the platform generates revenue

---

## 1. Philosophy

Monetization must:
- Not compromise credibility
- Be transparent to users
- Scale with user growth

---

## 2. Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscriptions
Linked to:
- User accounts

Description:
- Premium users pay a monthly or annual fee
- Benefits may include:
  - Ad-free experience
  - Early access to content
  - Exclusive opportunities

---

### 2. Sponsored Content
Linked to:
- Advertiser accounts
- Content records

Description:
- Clearly labeled sponsored articles
- Targeted to relevant user segments
- Subject to approval rules

---

### 3. Advertising
Linked to:
- User engagement metrics

Description:
- Display ads within free content
- Revenue based on impressions and clicks

---

### 4. API / Data Access (Future)
Linked to:
- Partner organizations

Description:
- Aggregated, anonymized insights
- Access to curated content feeds

---

## 3. What Is NOT Monetized

- User personal data
- Private behavior tracking
- Unverified promotions

---

## 4. Early-Stage Focus

Initial phase prioritizes:
- User growth
- Trust
- Content quality

Revenue scales after credibility is established.
