## Approved Sources
- Verified international media organizations
- Recognized African publishers
- Official RSS feeds from trusted outlets

## Restricted Content
- Clickbait or misleading headlines
- Misinformation or unverified claims
- Duplicate or recycled stories
- Hate speech or extremist content
- Political propaganda
- Scam or fraudulent material

---

## Content Categories
- News
- Politics
- Business
- Technology
- Sports
- Entertainment
- Education
- Youth Opportunities

---

## Language Policy
- English as the primary language
- Regional language support planned for later phases
- Content must remain neutral, factual, and respectful

---

## Administrative Control
Authorized administrators may:
- Remove content
- Pin priority stories
- Correct categorization errors
